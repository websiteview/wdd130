History

In the summer of 1995, a group of adventurous friends gathered by the roaring rivers of the Appalachian Mountains, where the thrill of whitewater rafting beckoned. Fueled by their love for the outdoors and a shared dream, they founded Rafting Co. With just a handful of inflatable rafts and a deep passion for adventure, they began guiding small groups through exhilarating rapids, introducing newcomers to the beauty and adrenaline of river life.

Over the years, the company grew, fueled by word-of-mouth from thrilled guests who cherished their unforgettable experiences. Rafting became known not just for its heart-pounding excursions, but also for its commitment to safety and environmental stewardship. The team embraced sustainable practices, educating participants on the importance of preserving the waterways they navigated.
